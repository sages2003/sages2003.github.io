my favorite places in D.C!

when I tell people I live in D.C. they always ask me what my favorite parts or places to go so here ya go.

1. the national mall(duh). specifically the National Gallery(i grew up going to muesuems with my mom, so this being a favorite is largely her influence).

2. georgetown. it's a really popular spot for tourists who are into shopping, but the waterfront is really gorgeous and you can see the Kennedy Center, and Virginia across the river. There are lots of spots you can sit, but there is also a variety of restaurants, a movie theater, and other little shops.

3. chinatown. chinatown has the Capital One Center, where all the big concerts and sports events. but chinatown is also home to the National Portrait Gallery, and a diverse handful of shops and restaurants. The architecture and unique color scheme makes the area exude culture and complexity
